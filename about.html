Matija Zavasnik

PHOTOGRAPHER AND VIDEOGRAPHER BASED IN SLOVENIA

I am the one who turns your moments into memories captured in photographs. I have been fascinated by photography since childhood. Alongside my technical skills and creativity in photography, I am known for my kindness, openness, and good communication. I am always ready to listen, understand others’ needs, and adapt to different situations, as I believe these qualities are key to successful collaboration and to creating authentic, emotionally impactful photographs.

I believe that a single photograph can tell a thousand words, which is why I prefer photographing over writing. So rather than reading, take a look at my albums and the stories they tell.
